Class - 8 Electric Truck

Powertrain thermal management system design

  • Designed and developed 42 parts across the powertrain thermal management system for Volvo’s VNR Electric
  • Incorporated 6 pumps, 4 heaters and 8 valves (3-way, 4-way and check valves) across the system to cater to 3 different flow rates, considering system requirements for traction voltage batteries, electric motors, DC Capacitors, E-PTO, DC-DC Convertors & Air Compressor
  • Collaborated with suppliers and CAE personnel to follow DFM principles and maintain continuous laminar flow across the system
